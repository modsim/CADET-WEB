Community Concept

We aim at establishing a community around the CADET project by providing free and open source software to both academia and industry. This allows other researchers to contribute back to the project by sharing improvements, extensions, and use cases.

Many groups working in chromatography modeling have developed their own simulation codes. However, code development is often stalled or abandoned when the person in charge leaves. This redundant effort can be saved by adopting CADET as a universal modeling framework and extending it to custom needs.

Moreover, a community-driven platform can potentially help in regulatory filings.

Current reference users of the CADET project include:

Prof. Anurag Rathore (Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi)
